LOGO FILES FOR DETECTX
======================

Place your logo files here:

1. logo.png       - For light mode (REQUIRED)
2. logo-dark.png  - For dark mode (OPTIONAL)

Both should be:
- PNG format with transparent background
- 512x512px or larger (square)
- High quality

If you don't provide logo-dark.png, the system will 
automatically use logo.png for both light and dark modes.

See DARK_MODE_LOGO.md for detailed instructions.
